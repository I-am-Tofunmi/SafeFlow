# SafeFlow 🛡️

> **AI-powered financial guardian for Nigeria's digitally-banked population**

SafeFlow protects and empowers everyday Nigerians navigating the digital finance ecosystem — turning vulnerable users into secure, informed, and financially resilient individuals.

---

## The Problem

Nigeria's informal economy ran on cash and caution for decades. When digital wallets arrived, millions finally joined the financial system — but a new wave of threats came with them: phishing scams, impulse spending, and fraud that can drain an account in seconds.

Despite all this digital activity, many users still can't build the credit history they need to move forward. They're online, but still **financially invisible**.

SafeFlow exists to make the room safe — not just open the door.

---

## What SafeFlow Does

SafeFlow combines **behavioural AI**, **predictive risk scoring**, and **personalised financial education** into one intelligent ecosystem, available via mobile app and USSD.

- 🚨 **Real-Time Scam Detection** — Monitors transaction patterns and incoming messages, flagging phishing links and blocking fraudulent transfers before money is lost
- 💰 **Smart Budgeting & Habit Building** — Impulse-spending alerts, savings goal tracking, and spending pattern insights in simple language
- 📊 **TrustScore** — Alternative credit scoring built from transaction consistency, savings patterns, and community endorsements — no collateral or paperwork needed
- 🏦 **Verified Financial Marketplace** — Curated directory of vetted lenders, insurers, and savings groups for safe financial expansion

---

## Why SafeFlow is Different

Most fintechs focus on fraud prevention **or** financial tracking. SafeFlow does both — and goes further.

- Understands local context — AI trained on Nigerian scam patterns, not generic global models
- Educates, not just blocks — explains *why* something is risky, building long-term user awareness
- Breaks the credit barrier — TrustScore creates a pathway to credit for millions excluded by traditional systems

## Built For

Nigeria's digitally-banked population — market traders, gig workers, students, small business owners — anyone who finally joined the financial system and deserves to stay safe in it.
