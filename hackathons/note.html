Password Requirements

Use a minimum of 12 characters - longer passwords are harder to crack.

Include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Avoid using personal information like your name, birthdate, or common words.

Don't reuse passwords from other accounts - each account should have a unique password.

Consider using a password manager to generate and store secure passwords.

Enhanced Security

Changing your password regularly helps protect your account from unauthorized access and potential security breaches.
